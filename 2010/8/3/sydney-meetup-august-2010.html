Sydney Meetup August 2010

Announcing the August meetup for Sydney aka rorosyd.

Tuesday August 10, at the Trinity Bar – 505 Crown St, Surry Hills, NSW 2010 (upstairs) 6:30 for a 7pm start!

We have 2 presentation length (15min) talks:
  • Ian Grant – (@MrFlungabunga) – Varnish
  • Richard Heycock – (@filterfish) – Smith
and 2 lightning talks (5min):
  • Keith Pitty – (@keithpitty) – RVM, Bundler and Ruby Tracker
  • Nick Sewell – (@nicsewell) – A whirlwind tour of Bayesian analysis

I’ve started going through the talks from earlier on this year that have been recorded and trying to make head and tail with the audio.
