Melbourne Ruby November - Analog Blog

Here’s the notes from last week’s Ruby meeting – the Analogue Blogue, and a list of people hiring Ruby developers.

Analog Blog

  • Rails Camp Perth
    • No New Memes
  • Next Australian Rails Camp is near Byron Bay in June.
  • Next Asia-Pacific Rails Camp is near Wellington in March.
  • Rails 3.0.2 – oh wait, 3.0.3
  • Rails 2.3.10
  • Rumours of a Ruby 1.8.8 – Engine Yard are the masterminds behind it. Final 1.8 release.
  • Phusion Passenger (modrails) 3.0 has been released
    • Better performance
    • Can run as a standalone server
    • Happier with RVM and Bundler
  • YOW and related workshops is next week!
    • Java, Ruby, Agile
  • Rails Conf 2011 – May in Baltimore
    • Speaker applications are now open

Hiring

  • Lonely Planet – several places available for variety of levels (mid to senior or a bright junior)
  • Red Bubble is hiring
  • Michael Morris is looking for designer-ish front-endy Rails-y person. Someone who can do everything.
  • SitePoint – Ruby developers. Get in touch.
    • Experienced Rails developers (different part of SitePoint.)
    • Ruby on Rails authors as well.
  • C3 – junior Rails developer. Includes such luminaries as Rob Postill and Mike Bailey.
  • Thoughtworks is always interested in good people.
  • realestate.com.au (REA) is looking for QAs and developers
