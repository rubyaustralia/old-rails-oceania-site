Canberra Ruby Crew February Meet

Following on from the Canberra Ruby Crew's first meetup last month Tim Riley has announced the second CRC get-together, and it's next Friday 22nd February.

If you can make it to Canberra and are interested in Ruby, Rails or fine-tuning your guitar hero skills I highly recommend going along.

From Tim's announcement on rails-oceania:

I write to announce the next meeting of the Canberra Ruby Crew.

  • When: Friday 22nd February, from 6:30pm (anytime from 6 is fine)
  • Where: My house (email me for the address)
  • What: Conversation, hacking, food & guitar hero
  • Who: All welcome

The last few meets have been very informal and a lot of fun. Come along and get involved in the greater Canberra Ruby community from the outset!
